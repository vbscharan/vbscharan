Product of array except itself
==============================
Ex:[1,2,3,4] -. [24,12,8,6]

Constraints - O(n) time and O(1) space, array can be empty, ca contains negative numbers and also zeros.



Solution
========
import math
m=input()
if(len(m)!=0):
    k=list(map(int,m.split(" ")))
    m=math.prod(k)
    for i in range(len(k)):
        if(k[i]==0):
           continue
        else:
            k[i]=(m//k[i])

    print(*k)
else:
    print("No input data")
